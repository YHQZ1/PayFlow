# PayFlow

### Financial infrastructure for businesses that mean business.

---

## The problem with payments today

Every business that wants to accept money online faces the same painful reality. You sign up with a payment provider, get handed a dashboard you barely understand, and hope for the best. When a payment fails, you find out from an angry customer — not from your system. When your accountant asks for a reconciliation report, someone spends a weekend pulling data from three different places.

Fraud happens quietly. Chargebacks arrive weeks later. And if you ever want to build something custom on top of your payment data — a dashboard, an automated payout flow, a fraud rule that fits your business — you're either locked out entirely or buried in documentation that assumes you have a dedicated engineering team.

PayFlow was built to change that.

---

## What PayFlow is

PayFlow is a payment platform for businesses that want more than a checkout button.

It gives you the tools to accept money, send money, track every rupee, and protect your revenue — all through a single integration, with full visibility into what's happening at every step.

Think of it as the financial backbone your business runs on.

---

## What you can do with PayFlow

**Accept payments your way**
UPI, cards, net banking, wallets — your customers pay however they prefer. PayFlow handles the routing, the retries, and the edge cases so successful payments are the default, not the exception.

**Send money at scale**
Pay vendors, run payroll, issue refunds, or disburse to partners — all from the same platform. Every outgoing payment is tracked, logged, and reconciled automatically.

**Know your numbers, always**
Every payment that flows through PayFlow is recorded using double-entry accounting — the same method banks use. Your books balance automatically. Your finance team gets real-time data instead of end-of-month surprises.

**Catch fraud before it catches you**
PayFlow scores every transaction in real time. Unusual velocity, suspiciously large amounts, repeated failures — the system flags them automatically and alerts you before a problem becomes a loss.

**Webhooks that actually deliver**
When something happens — a payment succeeds, a payout is sent, a subscription renews — PayFlow tells you instantly. If the delivery fails, it retries automatically. You get a record of every event, delivered or not.

**Build on top of your own data**
Your payment history, your ledger, your fraud signals — all queryable via API. Build the dashboard your business actually needs. Automate the workflows your team actually runs.
